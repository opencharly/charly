byte-identical exit/stdout/stderr/artifact verdict block after dispatching their op, and most
carried a local resultJSON/preview/pluginResult copy. All are replaced by sdk.VerbVerdict (sdk

different captured-stderr composition but drops its local resultJSON/preview for sdk.ResultJSON +
sdk.Preview. Grep self-test: zero `func resultJSON` / `func preview` / `type pluginResult` remain in
any plugin. VerbVerdict is byte-identical to the blocks it replaces (verified against cdp).

# 2026.189.0704 — dedupe the GPU-prereq skip reason (printed 3×) to once

A `requires_shared`/`requires_exclusive: [nvidia-gpu]` bed skipped on a GPU-less
host printed its skip reason THREE times: check_bed_run.go (the executor),
check_runner_cmd.go (the command), AND main.go's CheckSkippedError handler (via
the error Msg). Removes the two redundant inner prints — the reason now lives
solely on the returned `CheckSkippedError.Msg` and is printed ONCE at the top
level (main.go), the idiomatic return-error-print-once pattern. Skip semantics
unchanged (exit 3 / CheckSkippedExitCode; per-bed summary still written).

Verified live on the fresh binary: `charly check run check-cachyos-jupyter-ml-pod`
on this GPU-less host → exit 3, skip reason printed exactly ONCE (was 3×).

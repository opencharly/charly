## What moved (bodies, not shells)
The plugin candy now OWNS the whole `charly check` kong tree + output formatting + the AI-harness
orchestration: `box`/`live`/`run`/`feature run`/`run-local`/`sync-credential`/`scope`/`last-tag`/
`self-evaluate`/`list`/`report`/`note`/`list-ai`. The relocated BODIES (runCheckBed, RunHarness + the
iteration state machine, the run/run-local/synccreds/clone/watchdog/note/score leaves, PrintAgents)
drive the host over four seams. `charly check <sub>` dispatches in-proc via `dispatchInProcCommand` →
the plugin's `Invoke(OpRun)` → its kong grammar (verified end-to-end).
